Frequently Asked Questions

Ceremony

When is the ceremony?

The ceremony is at 13:00 on Friday August 9th

What is the location for the ceremony?

The ceremony will take place at St. Mary's of the Rosary Church, Nenagh E45 YH29

Photographs

There will be a group photograph before everyone leaves the church. (need to confirm photograph details)

There will be a QR code available at our Day 2 event, it would be great if you could send us any pictures/videos you take of our day.

Dress Code

The dress code is elegance, bring your A-Game 😉

Footwear

It will be a garden wedding so there may be times you will be on grass. For comfort it may be a good idea to pack some flat shoes. For any ladies wearing heels there will be heel plugs available in the ladies toilets. Block heels will also be a suitable choice.

Reception

Where is the reception venue?

The Reception will take place at Hermitage House Ballymacky E45 EH64

What time does the reception start at?

The reception will start with a champagne reception from 15.30

Will the reception be indoors or outdoors?

If the weather is fine, the champagne reception will be outdoors. However there will be arrangements made if not. The rest of the reception will be held indoors.

Can I use card at the reception?

There will be card facilities at the reception however the bar has asked if all guest could also bring cash on the off chance that there is an issue with the connection (we are in the Tipperary countryside)

Travel to and from the wedding venue

What is the best way to travel to the wedding venue?

It will be best to drive to the wedding venue. If you can carpool, that would be a great option too.

What is the best way to get home at the end of the night?

There is a bus organised that will be making trips throughout the night. It’s best to pre book the bus if you will be travelling a distance from Nenagh. Also best to travel with other guests going in the same direction.

Please note: cash will be needed for the bus service at the end of the night.

Contact number for the bus service: 086 3482276

Parking

There will be parking available on site. There will be someone on site to direct parking. You can come and collect your car the following day.

Day Two Arrangements

Where will day 2 events take place?

Day 2 will be in Tom’s Bar Templemore

What time are things kicking off on day 2?

Day 2 will kick off at around 4pm on August 10th

What is happening Day 2?

We are having music and a BBQ at Tom’s bar Templemore

What is the best way to travel?

Best to drive here or get a taxi. There isn’t any public transport available
